Studio Ghibli celebrates 20 years of magic with a theatrical re-release of Howl's Moving Castle! Sophie, a young girl lives an ordinary life working at her late father's hat store, until one day she befriends a wizard named Howl, who lives in a moving magical castle! However, the evil Witch of Waste finds this new budding relationship and grows jealous of Sophie. To regain Howl's love she curses young Sophie with an aging curse! Howl must use all of his magical talents and abilities to defeat the jealous witch and return Sophie to her former beauty!
